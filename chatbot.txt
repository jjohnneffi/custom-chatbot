Panimalar Engineering College, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is a Christian minority institution affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE. Known for its academic excellence, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering and management disciplines, including Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical, Electrical & Electronics, Civil Engineering, and MBA. The sprawling 133-acre campus provides a serene and eco-friendly learning environment with modern infrastructure, including digital classrooms, fully equipped laboratories, an extensive library, advanced research centers, and separate hostels for boys and girls. The college places a strong emphasis on innovation and industry collaboration, with research labs in AI, IoT, Robotics, and Data Science, as well as MoUs with top companies such as TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Amazon, and HCL.
